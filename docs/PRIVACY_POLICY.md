# Privacy Policy — Namma Madras

**Last updated:** April 2026

This policy describes how the Namma Madras Android app (“the app”) handles information. The app is built for offline-first humanitarian use in Greater Chennai.

## Data stored on your device

- **Resource directory:** Names, addresses, phone numbers, and descriptions of places are stored in a local SQLite database bundled with the app. This data is not sent to our servers by default.
- **Your choices:** Language, onboarding completion, optional Tamil-numeral preference, and saved places are stored on the device (local database and small key–value storage).
- **Location:** If you allow location access, coordinates are used on-device to sort results by distance. Location is not uploaded by the app.

## Optional features

- **Notifications:** If you grant notification permission, the system may be used in a future update to alert you about data updates. No notification content is described here until that feature ships.
- **Maps:** When online, map tiles are loaded from **OpenStreetMap** contributors via standard tile services; use is subject to the [OpenStreetMap Foundation privacy notice](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy) and the policies of the tile provider your device uses.
- **“Report incorrect info”:** Opens your email app with a pre-filled message to a maintainer address. Sending email is your choice and is governed by your email provider.

## What we do not do

- No advertising, no in-app purchases, no sale of personal data.
- No user accounts are required to use the app.
- No behavioural profiling in this version.

## Children

The app is intended for a general audience. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what the operating system provides for core app function.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support channel listed on the Google Play listing or the in-app feedback email.
