# Design context — Namma Madras

- **Audience:** Migrant workers, people in housing/food crisis, students, helpers — often on low-end Android, limited data, Tamil-first.
- **Jobs:** Find food/shelter/medical fast, call emergency numbers, understand rights — offline trust is mandatory.
- **Personality:** Calm, dignified, civic — not startup flashy, not clinical cold. Feels like a **trusted street map + NGO leaflet** digitised: utilitarian clarity with **warm human tone**. **Light UI** (warm off-white canvas, charcoal ink) with **teal** for trust/links and **coral** accents to align with sibling transit-style products; **red only** for emergency.
- **Constraints:** No heavy animation/blur; 48dp+ targets; WCAG AA; performance on 2GB RAM.

**Direction:** *Warm utilitarian (light)* — editorial hierarchy, left-edge category colour rails, restrained iconography (Ionicons outline system), varied vertical rhythm (time-first home, gradient context banner without glassmorphism, solid surfaces only).
